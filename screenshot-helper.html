Taking the Screenshots

To take screenshots on Windows:

  1. Press Windows + Shift + S to open the snipping tool
  2. Select the area you want to capture
  3. The screenshot will be copied to your clipboard
  4. Paste it into an image editor to save

Recommended screenshot dimensions: 1280x800 pixels
