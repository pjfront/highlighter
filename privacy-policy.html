Privacy Policy for Text Highlighter Counter

This privacy policy explains how the Text Highlighter Counter extension handles user data.

Data Collection

Text Highlighter Counter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or browsing data. All processing of text selections happens locally in your browser and is not sent to any external servers.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API to save your preferences (such as display settings) locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is only used to remember your settings between sessions.

Permissions

Text Highlighter Counter requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current tab for detecting text selections
  • storage: To save your preferences locally

Third-Party Services

The extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

External Links

The extension contains a link to Ko-fi (https://ko-fi.com/appvibin) in the options page. This link is provided for users who wish to support the development of the extension. Clicking this link will take you to the Ko-fi website, which has its own privacy policy and terms of service. We do not receive any personal data from Ko-fi about users who visit or make donations.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the extension and notify users through the Chrome Web Store listing.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our Ko-fi page at https://ko-fi.com/appvibin.
